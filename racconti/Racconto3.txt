Many years ago there lived an emperor who loved having beautiful new clothes so much that he used all his money to dress elegantly.
He did not care about his soldiers or going to the theater or walking in the woods, except to show off his new clothes.
He had a suit for every hour of the day and as it is usually said that a king is in council, so it was always said of him: "And in the dressing room!".
